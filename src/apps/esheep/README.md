# eSheep

## Purpose

**eSheep** is a nostalgic desktop pet that roams around your azOS desktop. Based on the classic 1995 "eSheep" for Windows, this application adds a touch of whimsy and life to your virtual workspace.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Pet**: The sheep walks, runs, jumps, and sleeps on top of your application windows and taskbar.
- **Physics-Aware**: The sheep interacts with the edges of your windows, treating them as platforms to walk or fall from.
- **Multiple Instances**: You can launch multiple sheep to create a flock on your desktop.
- **Animation Varieties**: Includes various classic animations and behaviors.

## How to Use

1.  Launch **eSheep** from the desktop or Start Menu.
2.  A sheep will appear on your desktop.
3.  Watch it interact with your windows and icons.
4.  Launch the app again to add more sheep.

## Technologies Used

- **PetJS**: Utilizes the PetJS library for character animation and behavior.
- **Screen Integration**: The sheep is rendered on a top-level transparent layer, allowing it to "walk" on other UI elements.
